title AMPS sequence

participant RabbitMQ
participant AMPS
participant Workload

[->RabbitMQ: Publish\nmessage
RabbitMQ->(1)AMPS: Queue\nmessage
activate AMPS
space -3
AMPS-->(1)RabbitMQ: Ack message
AMPS->AMPS: Register job\nin local manifest
AMPS->(1)Workload: Send job POST request
activate AMPS
activate Workload
space -3
Workload-->(1)AMPS: Ack job
entryspacing 3
group success
Workload->(1)AMPS: Job successful
deactivate Workload
AMPS->AMPS: Garbage collect job\nand accept new ones
deactivate AMPS
deactivate AMPS
end
group failure
activate AMPS
activate AMPS
activate Workload
Workload->(1)AMPS: Requeue
deactivate AMPS
deactivate Workload
AMPS->(1)RabbitMQ: Re-publish\nmessage
AMPS->AMPS: Garbage collect job\nand accept new ones
deactivate AMPS
deactivate AMPS
end

group timeout
activate AMPS
activate AMPS
activate Workload
AMPS->AMPS: Reconcile TTL\nand timeout job
deactivate AMPS

AMPS->(1)RabbitMQ: Re-publish\nmessage
AMPS->AMPS: Garbage collect job\nand accept new ones
deactivate AMPS
deactivate AMPS
end
deactivate Workload
